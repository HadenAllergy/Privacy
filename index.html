Haden Allergy App

Privacy Policy

Summary: The Haden Allergy App stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored on your device only:

  • Medications: Names, categories, and reminder settings you enter
  • Symptom journal entries: Dates, severity scores, and notes you record
  • App preferences: Your selected country/region and display settings

Location data:

  • When you grant permission, the app uses your device's GPS to fetch pollen counts and air quality data for your area
  • Your coordinates are sent directly to Open-Meteo (pollen/AQI API) and OpenStreetMap Nominatim (geocoding) to retrieve local data
  • Location data is not stored by the app beyond the current session
  • We do not have access to your location — it goes directly from your device to these third-party APIs

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Haden Allergy App does not:

  • Require or create user accounts
  • Collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind
  • Use advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • Use cookies
  • Send any data to our servers (we don't operate data collection servers)
  • Share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party

3. Third-Party Services

The app connects to two external services to provide pollen and location data:

Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com)

  • Purpose: Pollen counts, air quality index
  • Data sent: Latitude and longitude coordinates only
  • Their privacy policy: open-meteo.com/en/terms

OpenStreetMap Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org)

  • Purpose: Converting city names to coordinates (geocoding)
  • Data sent: City/location search text or coordinates
  • Their privacy policy: osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

Both services are accessed over encrypted HTTPS connections. No authentication tokens or user identifiers are sent with these requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and the app's local storage
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in encryption (iOS Data Protection)
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • You can delete all app data at any time using the "Clear All Data" button in the app's Info tab
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device

5. Children's Privacy

The Haden Allergy App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any user, it is compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6. Medical Disclaimer

The Haden Allergy App is for informational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Pollen counts are modeled regional estimates, not clinical-grade measurements. Always consult your physician or qualified healthcare provider with questions about a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it based on information from this app.

7. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it:

  • You have full control over your data at all times
  • You can view, modify, or delete any data within the app
  • You can delete all data using the "Clear All Data" function
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data
  • There is no account to delete because no accounts exist

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the effective date below. Material changes will also be noted in the app's Info tab.
